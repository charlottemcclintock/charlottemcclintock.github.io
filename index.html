Hi!

I'm Charlotte.

I’m an undergraduate student at the University of Virginia, studying applied statistics, human behavior, and politics/public policy. I’m hoping to do data analytics work for public policy organizations, advocacy groups, and non-profits to bring more empirical evidence, accountability for outcomes, and impact measurement to the social sector. I want to explore systems to find gaps and disparities and build solutions to make sure all people have equal access to opportunity. I’ve explored a lot of paths, looking for something both intellectually engaging and impactful, and I know this is what I want to do! I love solving problems, finding actionable insights, and creating change, especially when I get to explore a new question or make the world better in some small way. I look up to amazing people using data to inform and inspire like Hans Rosling and David McCandless.

I value gratitude, kindness, curiosity, and self-awareness. I love reading books and long-form journalism ( especially from Aeon), watching documentaries, sharing ideas, and solving problems. I love photography, especially when I get the chance to capture joy in action. I’m from Denver, Colorado and I love my home state for its big blue skies, beautiful outdoors, and great people.

Thanks for visiting me here! Please reach out if you want to chat!
