Hi!

I'm Charlotte.

I’m an undergraduate student at the University of Virginia, studying behavioral science and data science to understand how people make choices, both as individuals and in groups. I love pulling useful questions and insights from data to evaluate the impact of policies and I want to combine those insights with behavioral science findings to improve outcomes for marginalized communities. I’ve explored a lot of paths, looking for something both intellectually engaging and impactful, and I know this is what I want to do! I love projects and making things happen, especially when I get to build something (like this website!) or investigate a new question. I look up to amazing people using data to inform and inspire like Hans Rosling and David McCandless.

I value gratitude, kindness, curiosity, and self-awareness. I love reading books and long-form journalism (especially from Aeon), watching documentaries, sharing ideas, and solving problems. I love photography, especially when I get the chance to capture joy. I’m from Colorado and I love my home state for its big blue skies, beautiful outdoors, and great people.

Thanks for visiting me here! Please reach out if you want to chat!
