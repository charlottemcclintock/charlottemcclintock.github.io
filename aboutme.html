About Me

I’m an undergraduate student at the University of Virginia, studying behavioral science and data science to understand how people make choices, both as individuals and in groups. I’ve explored a lot of paths, and I know this is what I want to do because I find it intellectually engaging as well as deeply impactful work! I love projects and making things happen, especially when I get to build something (like this website!) or investigate a new question.

I value kindness, curiosity, and self-awareness. I love reading books and long-form journalism (especially from Aeon), watching documentaries, and sharing ideas and solving problems. I love photography, especially when I get the chance to capture joy. I’m from Colorado and I love my home state, big blue skies and beautiful outdoors, with some great people too.
