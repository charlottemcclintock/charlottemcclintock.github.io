Public Interest Data Lab 2018

Racial disparity and disproportionality in the Charlottesville Child Welfare System

The UVA Public Interest Data Lab is led by Michele Claibourn and jointly sponsored by the University of Virginia Library and the UVA Data Science Institute. The Lab provides data science experience to University of Virginia students in service of the public interest. I focused on disparity in foster care once children are placed in out of home care.
