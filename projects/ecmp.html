Mapping early childhood resources

Community Impact Lab

We are currently surveying early childhood service providers to better understand what resources exist to support low-income children and families in Charlottesville and Albemarle. We see the project as building on the foundation of the Early Childhood Fiscal Map but taking the question from a new angle, hoping to understand how resources are distributed by type of service and by population served. The completed map and report will be presented to the Early Childhood Funders Network on possible areas for local investment and will also be distributed to all participating early childhood providers.
