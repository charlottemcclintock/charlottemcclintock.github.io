Racial disparity in the Charlottesville Child Welfare System

Public Interest Data Lab 2018

I worked as a Lead Researcher in the Public Interest Data Lab on a project to investigate alleged racial disparity and disproportionality in the Charlottesville Child Welfare System. Working with Professor Claibourn on this project has radically changed the course of my life, and inspired me to pursue projects data science for the social good as a career.
