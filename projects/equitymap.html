Mapping and visualizing regional equity-related data

Charlottesville Area Equity Indicators

This project is the result of a number of conversations with community partners in the Charlottesville region about the accessibility of data related to regional equity to those with no formal data skills. Here we envision 'open data' not as a fixed state but as a process of opening data where the information becomes accessible to different people at in different mediums.

I worked as the primary developer and was responsible for the design and construction of the app, with contributions and guidance from others at the CommPAS Lab. The app as it stands now is in a beta form - each feature stands primarily to demonstrate what's possible with the technology.
