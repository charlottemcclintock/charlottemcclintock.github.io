How To Do Nothing


thoughts so far:
  • the attention economy: opposite of censorship is barrage of endless and useless information, chatter and discourse with no point or context
  • "To do nothing is to hold yourself still so you can perceive what is
  • connectivity vs. sensitivity: the structure of social media creates connectivity between people based on compatibility of ideas, instinctual responses, the opposite of deep listening. we are taught to have immediate takes with little information, instead of listening and trying to understand deeply.
  • Walden Two and Westword: being trapped constantly in the present, no context of history, as a way to keep docile - relationship to the digital news ecosystem, obscures bigger trends
  • Hannah Arendt - "age old temptation to substitute design for the political process"
