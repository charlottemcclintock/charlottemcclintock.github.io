origins of my digital garden


Welcome to my experiment in digital gardening!

In reading Jenny Odell's How To Do Nothing, I've been thinking a lot about how platforms shape my thought patterns and about what practices in my life I want to nurture and cultivate. For me, this website has always been a source of creativity - I started it when I knew absolutely no HTML or CSS and it's gone through many iterations and it's been such a joy to have a creative outlet for building and rebuilding and creating representations of myself.


inspired in part by:
  • Maggie Appleton's digital garden - love the layout, many tags, essay structure
  • Tom Critchlow's digital garden file structure
  • Gordon Brander's bag of tricks - I like connections between ideas piece
  • The structure of Anna Lowenhaupt Tsing's The Mushroom at the End of the World book - "a riot of short chapters"
